Support

Troubleshooting and contact information for Couples Alarm on iOS 26 or later.

An alarm did not work as expected

Keep using an alarm you already trust until you have tested Couples Alarm again. Open the app, confirm the saved sound and schedule, then create a near-future test alarm from the phone’s intended bedside position. Delete the test after it fires.

If the alarm is missing, uses the wrong sound, fires at the wrong time, or does not fire, contact support with the iPhone model, iOS version, Couples Alarm version, requested sound, scheduled time, and what happened. Do not include partner names or listening results.

Alarm access is off

Open Couples Alarm, find Alarm access, and tap Open Settings. Enable Alarm access, return to the app, and create the alarm again.

The alarm is not loud enough

  1. Open iPhone Settings → Sounds & Haptics. Move Ringtone & Alerts to the right and turn Change with Buttons off.
  2. Open Settings → Sounds & Haptics → Volume Limit and turn Limit Maximum Volume off.
  3. In Couples Alarm, open Settings → Alarm not loud enough? and run the 10-second real alarm test.

These changes also affect other calls, alerts, and speaker audio. If the real test is still not loud enough, do not rely on Couples Alarm as your only alarm.

We didn’t find a sound

This only works if the person who needs to wake up can hear a sound their partner cannot. Try the listening test again only if an answer seemed wrong. Use the iPhone speaker in a quiet room, disconnect headphones, and follow the volume instructions shown in the app. If the app still cannot find a sound that works for both of you, do not rely on Couples Alarm for that schedule. Keep using an alarm you already trust.

Reminder notifications are off

Free-month reminders are optional. Open iPhone Settings → Notifications → Couples Alarm to change them. Turning reminders off does not turn off alarms scheduled through AlarmKit.

Share beta feedback

Testing through TestFlight? Use the short beta feedback form for structured questions. Your answers are recorded when you submit the form so the developer can review them. For a crash or screenshot, use Send Beta Feedback in TestFlight.

Remove saved information

Delete an individual alarm from the alarm list. To remove both profiles, listening results, the confirmed sound, and every alarm from this iPhone, open Settings → Erase All Data and confirm. Retake listening test keeps alarms saved but pauses them until both partners confirm a new sound.

Contact support

Open Settings → Feedback & Support in Couples Alarm, or email couplesalarm.support@gmail.com. The in-app message includes the app and iOS versions for you to review before sending. Do not include partner names, listening results, or other sensitive information.
