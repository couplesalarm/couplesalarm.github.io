Couples Alarm

Support

Couples Alarm requires an iPhone running iOS 26 or later.

Alarm access is off

Open Couples Alarm, find Alarm access, and tap Open Settings. Enable Alarm access, return to the app, and create the alarm again.

No tone was suggested

Run the two-partner calibration again in a quiet room. Use the phone speaker at arm’s length and a low, comfortable volume. Do not use headphones or increase the volume to chase a frequency. If the app still finds no suitable bundled tone, keep using an alarm you already trust.

An alarm is missing or uses the wrong tone

Open Couples Alarm so it can reconcile its saved list with AlarmKit. Confirm the tone shown in the app, then create a near-future alarm and listen from the phone’s intended bedside position. Delete that alarm after the check. Do not rely on an unverified alarm.

Remove saved information

Delete individual alarms from the alarm list. To remove both profiles, their listening observations, the confirmed tone, and every saved alarm, open Settings, choose Retake listening test, and confirm.

Contact support

Open Settings → Contact Support in Couples Alarm, or email couplesalarm.support@gmail.com. Include the iPhone model, iOS version, Couples Alarm version, requested tone, and what happened. Do not include partner names, listening observations, or other sensitive information.
