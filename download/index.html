Currently in limited TestFlight beta

Couples Alarm is not publicly downloadable from the App Store yet. During beta, you need a Couples Alarm TestFlight invitation before you can install it.

Install TestFlight Then open the Couples Alarm invitation sent to your Apple Account. Open Couples Alarm in the App Store This permanent App Store link will allow installation after public release.

Use the frequency your partner sent

  1. Open Couples Alarm and finish the welcome pages.
  2. Enter the person waking first and the partner staying asleep.
  3. Tap I Have a Shared Frequency.
  4. Enter the frequency from the message, preview the sounds, and test the selected sound together.
  5. Complete the real bedside alarm check on this iPhone before relying on it.

A shared frequency avoids repeating the listening sweep. It does not guarantee the sound will work from a different iPhone or bedside position.
