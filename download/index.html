How it works

Built around one honest condition.

This only works if the person who needs to wake up can hear a sound their partner cannot. Couples Alarm checks that fit before either person is asked to rely on it.

  1. 01

    Choose who wakes first

    Set the roles for this schedule. Couples Alarm never silently swaps them.

  2. 02

    Listen separately

    Each partner completes two short listening turns using the iPhone speaker.

  3. 03

    Confirm together

    Test the suggested tone from the intended bedside position before setup completes.

one shared result

Designed not to guess

If the fit is not there, the app stops.

People, rooms, phones, and volume settings vary. If Couples Alarm cannot find a suitable bundled tone for the chosen schedule, it tells you to keep using an alarm you already trust.

Couples Alarm is an alarm utility. Its guided listening process is not a hearing test or medical assessment.

Private beta

Couples Alarm is not publicly downloadable from the App Store yet. Beta access requires a direct TestFlight invitation.
