Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Help home Support

Couples Alarm does not automatically collect or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties. The app has no account, backend, analytics, advertising, or third-party SDK.

Information stored on your iPhone

The app stores the following information only on the iPhone where it is entered:

  • Two partner names
  • Two listening observations for each partner
  • The bundled tone both partners confirm
  • Saved alarm schedules and settings

This information is used only to suggest a bundled tone, show the saved setup, and schedule alarms through Apple’s AlarmKit. It is not used for advertising, tracking, or a medical assessment.

Sharing

Couples Alarm does not share this app-local information. Alarm schedules are also represented in AlarmKit so iOS can deliver and manage the alarms.

Support email

If you choose Feedback & Support, Couples Alarm opens an email composer addressed to Couples Alarm Support. No profile, alarm, listening, or attachment data is added. The app and iOS versions are visibly prefilled in the editable message. The message is sent only if you choose Send.

Your email provider and the support inbox provider process the message according to their policies. Your email address and the information you choose to include may be retained as needed to respond and maintain support history; they are not used for advertising or tracking. Reply to the support conversation to request deletion.

Removing information

Delete an alarm from the alarm list to remove it. To remove both profiles, their listening results, the confirmed sound, and every alarm, open Settings, choose Erase All Data, and confirm. Retake listening test keeps alarms saved but pauses them until both partners confirm a new sound. Deleting the app also removes its app-local data.

Permissions

Couples Alarm requests Alarm access only when needed to schedule alarms. After setup, it may also request notification access for optional free-month reminders. Reminder notifications contain no names, schedules, or listening results. You can change either permission in iPhone Settings; turning reminders off does not turn off alarms scheduled through AlarmKit.

Questions

Visit Couples Alarm Support or email couplesalarm.support@gmail.com. Do not include partner names, listening observations, or other sensitive information.
