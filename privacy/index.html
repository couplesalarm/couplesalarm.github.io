Couples Alarm

Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026

Couples Alarm does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties. The app has no account, backend, analytics, advertising, or third-party SDK.

Information stored on your iPhone

The app stores the following information only on the iPhone where it is entered:

  • Two partner names
  • Two listening observations for each partner
  • The bundled tone both partners confirm
  • Saved alarm schedules and settings

This information is used only to suggest a bundled tone, show the saved setup, and schedule alarms through Apple’s AlarmKit. It is not used for advertising, tracking, or a medical assessment.

Sharing

Couples Alarm does not share this information. Alarm schedules are also represented in AlarmKit so iOS can deliver and manage the alarms.

Removing information

Delete an alarm from the alarm list to remove it. To remove both profiles, their listening observations, the confirmed tone, and saved alarms, open Settings, choose Retake listening test, and confirm. Deleting the app also removes its app-local data.

Permissions

Couples Alarm requests Alarm access only when needed to schedule alarms. You can change that permission in iPhone Settings.

Questions

Visit Couples Alarm Support or open a public support request. Do not include partner names, listening observations, or other sensitive information in a public request.
